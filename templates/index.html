About the project

A summary of the project

    ____________________________

    Kiwi is a company of delivery services using kiwibots. The challenge of working with robots is self driving, something that the company believes can be solved completely using cameras, only vision. After trying several different approaches for the first years, kiwi have settled for a vision based detection system.

The project path

A summary of the path we develop to achieve the goal

  • 1.Simulator

    ____________________________

    The first challenge we faced in our project was to create a simulator. The idea was to simulate an enviroment with people walking around in random ways. This was developed in Unity.

    ____________________________

  • 2.DATA

    ____________________________

    Our second challenge was to collect data, in this case our data are images. Once we had the simulator working what we did was to run it and to capture some photos of the "people" while they where walking in different position. On a first dataset we took 1.600 images and at the end wefinished using around 3.600 images.

    ____________________________

  • 3. The Model

    ____________________________

    Our third challenge was to create a model that would be trained to learn and recognized if an object in an image is located near, medium or far from the kiwibot view. To train the model we divided the dataset images in 80-20. 80% of the pictures where used to train the model and the 20% left where used to test.

    ____________________________

  • 4.Landing page

    ____________________________

    Once we had the model working well, our fourth challenge was to implement our program in a landing page. To join the backend with the frontend

    ____________________________

  • 5.Deployment

    ____________________________

    Finally, once we had the backend and the landing page ready our last challenge was to find a server to make the deployment process in wich finally we decided to do it in Heroku platform.

    ____________________________

What is Acqua tech?

Acqua tech is a group conformed by software developers participants from Holberton School #cohort10

Holberton School is a software development academy in wich every student studies for 9 months the foundations of programming. After 9 months of intense sudies the participants have to present a final project in wich they can develop and apply all what they have learned during this period.

Thinking on the final porject, Holberton School made some alliances with some companies in wich every participant can work in a real project to develop with them.
